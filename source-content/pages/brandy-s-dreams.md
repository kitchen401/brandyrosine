Brandy's Dreams​

Where she was headed

Brandy genuinely cared about people. She gravitated toward people who were through tough times or experiencing challenges in life for this reason. She was a good listener and had a way of giving advice that was wise beyond her years.

Brandy had an interest in understanding human behavior. Why do people do the things they do? She wanted to head down a career path that would allow her to explore this further; turn her natural abilities and interest into a tool that would help others.
After high school, Brandy enrolled at Youngstown State University and was studying psychology and sociology.
