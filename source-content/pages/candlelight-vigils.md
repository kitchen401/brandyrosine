Candlelight Vigils​

Annual Vigils

Every year on or around May 17th, we hold a vigil in honor of Brandy. They are held at the memorial bench on the campus of Youngstown State University.

The public is welcome and it is typically between 20 - 30 people. We usually say a prayer, light candles, share memories, and do a (biodegradable) balloon launch at the end.
